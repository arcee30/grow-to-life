Here's a list of skills and jobs we need: gardening, nutrition classes, food distribution, fundraising, canvassing, beekeeping, event planning,
grant writing, graphic/web design, social media, art + crafts, healthy cooking, etc.

If you are new to Grow to Life and have been approved to volunteer with us, please complete the Volunteer Waiver Form, which will direct you to DocuSign. You must fill out this waiver in order to start volunteering. If you are a regular volunteer and would like to keep track of your hours, please fill out this form each time you volunteer with Grow to Life. Upon approval of the volunteer hours you submitted, you will receive an email from Grow to Life confirming your hours. To check how many hours you have, please email us at growtolifeorg@gmail.com.
