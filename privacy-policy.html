Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10th, 2023

Grow to Life provides this Privacy Policy statement to make you aware of our guidelines and security practices and to inform you about the way your information is collected and used. We encourage you to read it carefully.

Awareness

Grow to Life is committed to protecting your online privacy and security. We have structured our website to allow you to visit us on the Web without identifying yourself or revealing any personal information. Once you choose to provide us with personally identifiable information, it will be used only to support your relationship with Grow to Life  and you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

What We Collect

Within Grow to Life’s website are pages that allow you to make donations and sign up for email correspondence. Grow to Life collects personally identifiable information such as your name and contact information. Our subsequent communications with you are used to continuously maintain the quality of our services and we may ask you to provide us with information regarding your demographics and contact preferences.

How We Use It

Grow to Life uses your information to understand your needs and to provide you information about our events and programs.

Who We Share It With

Grow to Life does not sell, rent, or lease your personally identifiable information to third parties. Unless we have your permission or are required by law, we will only share your personal information with Grow to Life entities and/or business partners who are acting on our behalf to complete the activities described above. Grow to Life entities and/or business partners, including those located in other countries, are governed by our privacy policies with respect to the use of this information and are prohibited from using your personally identifiable information except to provide these services to Grow to Life. Additionally, they are required to maintain the strict confidentiality of your information.

Accuracy and Access

Grow to Life strives to keep your personally identifiable information accurate. To review or correct your personal information, contact us at growtolifeorg@gmail.com to make any changes.

Security

Grow to Life secures your information in a controlled secure environment to protect against unauthorized access, use or disclosure and maintains data accuracy to ensure the appropriate use of information.  We have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

Computer Tracking and Cookies

Cookies are used to determine the success of Grow to Life’s website and marketing campaigns as well as personalize your online experience. This allows us to be good stewards of the donations we receive by helping us determine our online effectiveness.

A cookie is a series of data characters that, when programmed into a website, is placed by the web server into the browser’s application folder on your computer. Once placed onto your machine, the cookie will allow the website to “recognize” you as a unique individual. Cookies do not collect data from your hard drive, nor can they be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer.

In addition to cookies, we use other technologies, including Google Analytics, to gather information on how our users interact with our website which helps us efficiently monitor our website and marketing campaigns. These technologies, for example, show us popular pages, conversion rates, click-throughs, and other information that can be used to improve your experience. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of Grow to Life or other Websites you may visit.

Problems or Complaints with Grow to Life Online Privacy Policy

If you have a complaint or concern about Grow to Life compliance with this privacy policy, please contact us at growtolifeorg@gmail.com.
