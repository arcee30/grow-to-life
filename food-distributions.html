Food Distributions

FIGHTING FOOD INSECURITY IN CHAPEL HILL

Since 2014, Grow to Life has collaborated with the Town of Chapel Hill Parks and Recreation to host complimentary food distributions to combat food insecurities with the aid of community members and local college students. Anyone is allowed to come and receive food! We look forward to serving in 2022 and beyond.
