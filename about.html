A Word from the Founder

Hi! My name is Ms. Makeda Ma'at, and I'm the founder and executive director of Grow to Life. *Notes about why you created Grow to Life*

‍We are blessed to operate a completely free food distribution site for the citizens of Chapel Hill and surrounding areas. Our direct distribution site has offered a drive-thru from conception, making it convenient and simple for neighbors to have access to nutritious meals.

We also operate as a short-term food pantry providing groceries routinely to people in need of emergency food, and providing food and other supplies to community members as needed. By providing hunger relief in a friendly non-judgmental manner, we desire to work in harmony for the good of all.

In addition to our food distribution work, we maintain garden spaces serving and educating local youth. Our community garden classes have helped our youth understand where their food comes from and filled them with zeal to build more sustainable communities.
