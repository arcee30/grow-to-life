Grow to Life

Grow to Life is a Black-led grassroots community movement with the mission “to serve GOD by serving others” and a vision of “the community serving the community”. Grow to Life has three main ways we work across projects—in everything we do, we strive to  Grow, Give, and Educate.

Our Themes

Grow

Grow to life is centered on the life-giving nature of food and how it is grown from seed to fruit. Our efforts have provided fresh healthy food and education to the community including the nutritional value.

Give

Grow to Life provides hunger relief via complimentary food distributions in the Chapel Hill/Carrboro area. We are deeply rooted in the community providing a welcoming space for people of all walks of life. High school and college volunteers are a key component.

Educate

Grow to Life provides educational classes in healthy alternative food preparation, nutrition, and agriculture. Our programs impact by encouraging new healthy lifestyle behaviors and improved educational outcomes for youth.

The Landing Blossom

A youth garden located in Carrboro, North Carolina, dedicated to educating children about gardening and food

Why Start Volunteering?

Volunteering at Grow to Life is not just about the organization benefiting from your time and skills; it's also about your personal growth, the connections you make, and the impact you have on the world around you. Embrace this opportunity to make a difference while discovering new passions and cultivating a greater sense of purpose.

  • Do good for others and the community

  • Increase your self-confidence and happiness

  • Make a difference to the lives of others

  • Feel valued and part of a team

Interested in Sponsoring Us?

At Grow to Life, we organize regular food distribution events where we provide nourishing meals and essential groceries to individuals and families in need. We have served thousands of individuals since our inception, and we aspire to expand our efforts further and reach even more people who are struggling with food insecurity. To achieve this, we are seeking the support of sponsors to support our cause.

Your sponsorship would primarily contribute to funding the purchase of food for our distribution events. In return for your sponsorship, we offer various benefits to showcase your commitment to social responsibility and community service. These benefits include prominent branding and recognition at our events, acknowledgement on our website and social media platforms, and more.
