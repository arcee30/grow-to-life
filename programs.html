Healthy Alternative Preparation & Nutrition Education

We offer many community opportunities to learn alternative methods for preparing great tasting food full of protein and vitamins necessary to maintain a healthy diet. Whether it be through cooking classes guided by local chefs or beekeeping classes to pick up a new hobby that teaches you about the nutritional value of honey, we have something for you.

Garden Work / Educate Day

In Work Educate Days, we aim to teach gardening skills to community members. Together we work to prepare the land by weeding and clearing the space, and when it's ready we begin to plant. If you're interested in volunteering with us please check out our events page for upcoming Work Educate days.

Sarah Harrington Elder Program

In honor of our dedicated volunteer, Sarah Harrington, Grow to Life is developing a program to assemble and deliver supply boxes to individuals 65 years and older. Please fill out our interest form if you, or someone you know, is interested in receiving a supply box in the future.
